About The Event

RecHERsion is a women-focused competitive coding event designed for students who want to improve algorithmic problem solving in a high-energy, professional environment.

What participants can expect

  • Curated problem sets with balanced difficulty and clear statements.
  • Structured timeline with chapter-led communications and support.
  • Pan-campus visibility, recognition, and growth opportunities.
  • An inclusive and motivating contest atmosphere designed for women in tech.

Eligibility & Rules

Please review all contest requirements carefully before participating.

Eligibility

  1. Open to undergraduate students (Engineering or related fields).
  2. Only women participants allowed.
  3. Inter-college participation permitted.

Rules & Regulations

  1. All code must be written independently; plagiarism is strictly prohibited.
  2. Use of AI tools, online forums, or external help is not allowed.
  3. Only individual participation; no team collaboration permitted.
  4. Participants must use only their registered HackerRank account.
  5. All submissions must be completed within the 3-hour time limit.
  6. Communication with other participants during the contest is prohibited.
  7. Any rule violation or suspicious activity will lead to disqualification.
  8. Organising team’s decision will be final and binding.
